Former Members of Casa de Cinema

The following were also members of Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre, during its first phase (1987-1991) (these pictures are from this time):


Angel Palomero
A theatre actor and director, as well as screenwriter, currently based in Rio de Janeiro, where he teaches theatre direction at UNIRIO and is a member of the managing board of ABRACE, Associação Brasileira de Pesquisa e Pós-Graduação em Artes Cênicas (Brazilian Association of Research and Graduate Studies in Drama).

Carlos Gerbase

Director and writer. PhD in Communications at PUC/RS, coordinator of the Audiovisual Production Program at that university. Director of the feature films VERDES ANOS/GREEN YEARS (1984), TOLERÂNCIA/TOLERANCE (2000), SAL DE PRATA/SILVER SALT (2005) and 3 EFES/3 F´s (2007) and also the Super-8 feature INVERNO/WINTER (1983). Writer and director of the 35 mm shorts INTERLÚDIO/INTERLUDE (1983), PASSAGEIROS/PASSENGERS (1985), AULAS MUITO PARTICULARES/VERY PRIVATE LESSONS (1988), O CORPO DE FLÁVIA/FLAVIA’S BODY (1990), DEUS EX-MACHINA (1995) and SEXO & BEETHOVEN - O REENCONTRO/SEX & BEETHOVEN – THE REUNION (1997). He co-wrote screenplays for Globo TV, including the miniseries MEMORIAL DE MARIA MOURA (1994) and ENGRAÇADINHA (1995). He also directed specials for television, including O COMPRADOR DE FAZENDAS (2001) for Globo TV and FAUSTINA (2002) for RBS TV. He is currentl preparing the screenplay of TRÊS GAROTAS E UM ET / THREE GIRLS AND AN E.T.
• Contact: gerbase@portoweb.com.br

Luciana Tomasi

Executive Producer. Responsible for the production of the features TOLERÂNCIA/TOLERANCE (2000), HOUVE UMA VEZ DOIS VERÕES/TWO SUMMERS (2002), O HOMEM QUE COPIAVA (2003), MEU TIO MATOU UM CARA (2005), SAL DE PRATA/SILVER SALT (2005) and SANEAMENTO BÁSICO, O FILME/SASIC SANITATION, THE MOVIE (2007)of shorts such as PASSAGEIROS (1985), O CORPO DE FLÁVIA (1990), MEMÓRIA (1990), BATALHA NAVAL (1992), DEUS EX-MACHINA (1995), SEXO & BEETHOVEN - O REENCONTRO (1997) and O OITAVO SELO (1999) and the TV series CENA ABERTA (2003), INCIDENTE EM ANTARES (section shot in Rio Grande do Sul, 1994) and LUNA CALIENTE (1998). She was production manager in INVERNO/WINTER (feature, super-8, 1983). She was production coordinator of the TV programs in the electoral campaigns of PT from 1992 to 2002. She coordinated the production of several TV specials, particularly the series CONTOS DE INVERNO (2001 and 2002). She is currently preparing the production of MATÉRIA DE MEMÓRIA/THE MATERIAL OF MEMORY.
• Contact: luli@casacinepoa.com.br
