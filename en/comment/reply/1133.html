UMA HISTÓRIA DE VERDADE (True History)

(Betacam, 58 min, color, 1998)

How the Partido dos Trabalhadores (Workers' Party) of Rio Grande do Sul came to win the state’s government. The best moments of Olívio Dutra and the Popular Front's victorious 1998 campaign in the elections for state government. The history of the party in the state, with images from all campaigns since 1988.

Directors: Angela Pires and Ana Luiza Azevedo

Executive Producers: Luciana Tomasi and Nora Goulart
Written by: Carlos Gerbase and Jorge Furtado
Cinematographers: Roberto Henkin and Alex Sernambi
Art Director: Fiapo Barth
Production Coordinator: Débora Peters
Editor: Giba Assis Brasil

A Partido dos Trabalhadores/RS Production of a Casa de Cinema PoA Realization

FULL CAST AND CREW
