Casa de Cinema's shorts

(DVD, 4 x 150 min aprox, 2005)

Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre is launching a box with 4 DVDs. They include over 40 short fiction films, documentaries and TV specials. The films were directed by national and international award winning filmmakers Ana Luiza Azevedo, Carlos Gerbase and Jorge Furtado. The collection also includes the DVD Outras Histórias (Other Stories), with short-films directed by guest filmmakers. All the short-films are subtitled subtitles in four different languages. Exclusive making-of footage and extras.

Historic edition.
