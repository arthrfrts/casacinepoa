CONTOS DE INVERNO (Winter Stories)

(Digital beta, 4 x 23 min, color, 2001)

The most important TV fact of Rio Grande do Sul in 2001. Regional drama, local actors, writers and directors, local color and themes.

Directors: Gilberto Perin, Ana Luiza Azevedo, Carlos Gerbase and Fabiano de Souza.

Writers: Jorge Furtado, Carlos Gerbase, Giba Assis Brasil, Luis Fernando Veríssimo, Glênio Póvoas, Ana Luiza Azevedo, Fabiano de Souza and Emiliano Urbim.
Cinematographers: Alex Sernambi and Jorge Boca.
Art Directors: Élcio Rossini and Rogério Nazzari.
Music by: Ricardo Severo, Flávio Santos and Leo Henkin
Editing Supervisor: Giba Assis Brasil.
Production Coordinator: Aline Rizzotto.
Executive Producers: Nora Goulart and Luciana Tomasi.

RBS TV DRAMA STAFF
Special Programs Coordinator: Gilberto Perin
Operation Manager: Norton Marcon
Production Manager: Alice Urbim
Responsible Director: Raul Costa Jr.
