OTHER ADDRESSES OF CINEMA OF RIO GRANDE DO SUL
APTC-ABD/RS - The Association of Professionals and Technicians in Cinema - the Association of Documentary Filmmakers, the organ which represents the filmmakers of Rio Grande do Sul. Their site carries the latest news bulletins of the association, instructions on how to become a member and contact the board of directors and other members, as well as complete and up-to-date information about all film festivals in Brazil.

Prêmio RGE/Governo RS de Cinema - The biggest film prize in Brazil
SIAV/RS - Audiovisual Industry Union of Rio Grande do Sul
SATED/RS - Rio Grande do Sul Artists and Technicians’ Union
