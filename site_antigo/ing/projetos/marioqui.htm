QUINTANA E AS MUSAS
(Quintana and the muses)

TV special directed by Márcio Schoenardie. Episode of the "100 anos de Quintana" (100 years of Quintana) series for RBS TV.

On the roof of a building in downtown Porto Alegre, someone claiming to be Zeus introduces us to his nine daughters, the muses, and looks for the inspiration of each one as it can be seen in different fragments of Mario Quintana’s poetry.

Being finished.
