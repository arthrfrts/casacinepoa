UM DOS TRÊS
(One of the three)

Serial segment for Globo network’s "Fantástico" program
General supervisor: Jorge Furtado
Director: Marcio Schoenardie

 

Somebody tells a personal story, something interesting that happened at work, at home, during a trip, on a vacation, on the street, anything. Then someone else comes along telling the same story, absolutely the same – with a few different details, but with the same ending. Last, a third person tells the same story, equal in everything – almost.

Or the three alternate, telling the same story, repeating the same details, phrases, gestures – actually, the three are telling the same story all along.

It isn’t possible: of course one of these people is lying. Better yet: at least two of them are lying. The program guarantees (and, in the end, proves it): ONE OF THE THREE is telling the truth, the others are actors hired to fool you. The fun is finding out which is which.

UM DOS TRÊS / One of the Three premiered Sunday, November 6, 2005, on "Fantástico". And it is still showing every Sunday, as a special segment on Globo TV’s weekly electronic variety magazine.

UM DOS TRÊS / One of the Three is a production by Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre, with writing and direction supervision by Jorge Furtado, scene direction by Márcio Schoenardie, production by Bel Merel and direction assistance by Janaína Fischer.
