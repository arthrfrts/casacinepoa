O AMOR NOS ANOS 90
(Love in the nineties)
(35 mm, 12 min, color, 1989)
 
Five interpretations of love and its near future, in very short episodes. (1) Cupids are illegal, and whoever is caught with one can be incriminated. (2) Two people meet and desire each other in an art gallery. (3) A couple goes into crisis on the day the AIDS vaccine is discovered. (4) The last flight is departing, as in Casablanca. But this time there is only one ticket. (5) A man and a woman separated by an aerial, a desire and a crime that is to be committed.

Reviews

"LOVE IN THE NINETIES is the 5 in 1 shorts (5 very short shorts making up one longer short). Expectations are running high about the short film to be shown today (at the Brasília Film Festival). After all, it is a production of the Casa de Cinema, which took Gramado by storm with ISLAND OF FLOWERS. It is a film made by the students of an extension course promoted by the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul and ministered by Casa de Cinema."
(Maria do Rosário Caetano, JORNAL DE BRASíLIA, 04/11/89)
