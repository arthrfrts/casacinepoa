HISTORY

Filmed in Porto Alegre and Sao Leopoldo/RS, from August 20 to September 14, 1983.
Premiére at the Gramado Festival, April 9, 1984.
Opened in Porto Alegre, theaters Scala and Coral, May 25, 1984.
