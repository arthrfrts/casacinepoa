PROJETO PULEX
(The Pulex Project)
(16 mm, 10 min, color, 1991)
 
An animation about Brazil in the year 2013; a country at the height of social crisis. In order to stop the increase of the low income population, the inner circle of government finds a solution: the flea.
 
 

PHOTO by Norton Cardia

Director: Tadao Miaqui

Art Director: Kyoko Yamashita
Music by: Cláudio Bonder
Production Coordinator: Fernanda Verissimo
