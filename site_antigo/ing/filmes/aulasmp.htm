AULAS MUITO PARTICULARES
(Very private lessons)
(35 mm, 10 min, color, 1988)
 
In order to receive his father's inheritance, an 18-year old boy has to take a course. On his first lesson, a kiss. On the second one, a woman's body. On the third one, the primer of the sex act. Other lessons will come, and the boy will still have to do the final exam.
