DONA CRISTINA PERDEU A MEMÓRIA
(Dona Cristina lost her memory)

STILL IMAGES
by Ana Luz
Antonio (Pedro Tergolina)
is 8 years old and he lives
in a house with a garden. 
 Dona Cristina (Lissy Brock)
is 80 years old and she lives
in an old people's home.
One day, they discover
that they are neighbours
across the fence. 
Antonio discovers
a world where time
can repeat itself.
Dona Cristina believes
that Antonio can help her
find her lost memories. 
And they become
friends forever.
At least until tomorrow.
