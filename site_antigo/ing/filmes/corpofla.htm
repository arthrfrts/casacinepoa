O CORPO DE FLÁVIA
(Flavia's body)
(35 mm, 15 min, color, 1990)
 
A fifteen year-old girl is beaten by her father and seeks shelter at a friend's house. From behind the tears and sobbing she observes her friend's father. He feels sorry for a child, but she is a woman. Tonight, they will have to discover the difference between passion and compassion.
