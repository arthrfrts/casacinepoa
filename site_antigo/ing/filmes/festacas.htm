FESTA DE CASAMENTO
(The Wedding)
(35 mm, 11 min, color, 1990)
 
A country wedding outside the city of Rio Grande, 1930. Among the guests is the bride's lover with his pregnant wife. During the party, the lovers decide to elope. The groom gathers together a group of friends and they set out to seek revenge.
