O VELHO DO SACO
(The Sandman)
(35 mm, 14 min, color, 1999)
 
Ricardo is 10 years old, he lives in a small town in Rio Grande do Sul and has nightmares about the Sandman, "who rips out the eyes of naughty children", It is not easy to distinguish truth from lies when the loss of innocence is confused with the loss of sight.
