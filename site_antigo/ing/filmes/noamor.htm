NO AMOR
(For free)
(35 mm, 11 min, p&b, 1982)
 
A group of craftspeople sell wallets, belts, bags and sandals in a downtown street, but the production is disorganized and not very lucrative. A young entrepreneur decides to invest in the kids, he splits them into work gangs and reduces their free time. In exchange, he offers them a great place to live. The "firm" starts making a profit, but the workers’ earnings are still poor.
