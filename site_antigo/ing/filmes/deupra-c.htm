HISTORY

Filmed in Porto Alegre, with aditional scenes in Garopaba/SC and Capao da Canoa/RS, from April to October, 1980. Show "Deu pra ti anos 70" pre-shot in December 22, 1979.
Opened at the Gramado Super-8 Festival, March 24, 1981.
