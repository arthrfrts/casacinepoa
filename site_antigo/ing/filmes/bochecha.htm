O BOCHECHA
(Cheeks)
(Digital Beta, 19 min, color, 2002)
 
Episode of the series "Winter Stories 2002", on RBS TV. 

A couple live together without ever having a fight, an argument, a quarrel. In order to prevent their life falling into the doldrums, the wife has a fantasy. And she will do whatever she can to make it come true.
