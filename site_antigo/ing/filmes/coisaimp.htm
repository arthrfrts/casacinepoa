A COISA MAIS IMPORTANTE DA VIDA
(The Most important thing in life)
(35 mm, 12 min, color, 1990)
 
A finds out that B is cheating on him, so he pays C to kill B. D tries to dissuade C not from carrying it out, and asks E for help. But each person has a different idea of what the most important thing in life is. So, E refuses to help D to prevent C from killing B in exchange from A’s money.
