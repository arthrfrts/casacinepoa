O ÚLTIMO DESEJO DO DR. GENARINHO
(Dr. Genarinho's last wish)
(Digital Beta, 18 min, color, 2002)
 
Episode of the series "Winter Stories 2002", on RBS TV. 

An old man lives by himself in Porto Alegre and has a strange wish for his funeral. This wish will lead a young man to struggle with difficulties to fulfil the last wish of the man he believes to be his father.
