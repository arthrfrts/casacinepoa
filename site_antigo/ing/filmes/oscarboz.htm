OSCAR BOZ
(Digital Beta, 12 min, color, 2004)
 
 
Episode of the "Umas Velhices" (Some Old Ages) series by SESC-SP Audiovisual Center.

Jorge Furtado talks to Oscar Boz, a pioneer of family movies. He shows scenes shot by Boz in the fifties in its original format and after they have been re-worked in a computer, leading to considerations about memory and a dialogue between past and future. Oscar Boz shot in Caxias do Sul and in the Rio Grande do Sul beaches from 1951 to 1957.
