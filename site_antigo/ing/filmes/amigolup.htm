AMIGO LUPI
(Our friend Lupi)
(16 mm, 15 min, color, 1992)
 
The life of the most famous popular composer of Rio Grande do Sul, Lupicínio Rodrigues (1914-1974), is remembered through his songs and friends' testimonies.
