Survey: How far does your “Tolerance” go?

A controversy on the set: how would the members of the crew of “Tolerance” react if they found out their partner had an extra-marital affair? Let’s find out how tolerant they are:

“I am tolerant so long as I don’t know about it.”
Denise Garcia – production coordinator

“I am intolerant. Fidelity is crucial.”
Alfredo Barros – 2nd assistant director

“I am tolerant depending on the relationship. As I have been through a situation like that, I had to adapt. First I had the worst reaction you could imagine, but later I even came to understand it.”
Cynthia Caprara – Casting

I am intolerant. The proof of it is that if I filmed a Turkish version* of “Tolerance” it would be a short, as the adulterous woman would be stoned right at the beginning.”
Dennison Ramalho – Video assist operator

* As foreign films are not allowed in Turkey, they produce versions of North American movies that are adapted to the norms of Islam. Whoever has seen the Turkish versions of Star Wars and The Exorcist swears that there is nothing worse in terms of trash movies.

“As long as I don’t know anything, my “Tolerance” goes a long way.
Fiapo Barth – art director

“My “Tolerance” goes as far as my partner does not cheat me more than I do her.”
Bel - continuity
