NEGRO BONIFÁCIO
(Bonifacio, the nigger)
(Digital Betal, 33 min, color, 2003)
 
Episode of the "Cena Aberta" (Open Scene) series for TV Globo.

A love triangle set in the traditional gaucho pampa. Bonifácio and Nadico race for the love and care of Tudinha. Afterwards, at the dance, when it comes to paying the bet, a knife fight decides the dispute. To play the story, an actor from Bahia and an actress from Rio discover the Brazilianness of the rural culture of Rio Grande do Sul.
