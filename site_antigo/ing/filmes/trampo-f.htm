STILL IMAGES
by Rodrigo Baleia


 


Raquel (Júlia Barth)
is a teenager
like any other.
At the club,
she practices
diving.
When she’s not in school,
all she thinks of are
overpasses and suicides.
Her classmates
never understand
what she says.
Raquel’s games
may turn out
badly.
Or maybe not.
Filming at
Otávio Rocha
overpass.
The director 
Fiapo Barth
during the shoot.
