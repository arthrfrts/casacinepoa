VICIOUS
(Vicious)
(35 mm, 15 min, p&b, 1988)
 
Sid and Nancy: an impossible love between impossible beings. Anguish explodes in bloody delirium. Shattered by contradictions, the desperate search is the only way. But every crime has its punishment. The synthesis between agony and ecstasy is usually fatal.
