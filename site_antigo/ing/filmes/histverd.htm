UMA HISTÓRIA DE VERDADE
(True History)
(Betacam, 58 min, color, 1998)
 
How the Partido dos Trabalhadores (Workers' Party) of Rio Grande do Sul came to win the state’s government. The best moments of Olívio Dutra and the Popular Front's victorious 1998 campaign in the elections for state government. The history of the party in the state, with images from all campaigns since 1988.
