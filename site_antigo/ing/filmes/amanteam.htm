O AMANTE AMADOR
(The Amateur adulterer)
(Digital Beta, 23 min, color, 2001)
 
Episode of the series "Winter Stories" of RBS TV.

Everardo, a perfect husband, is thinking of committing adultery for the first time, but he is scared of his wife’s jealousy. A more experienced friend suggests that Everardo should start by making up a fake adultery, just to check his wife’s reaction and his capacity to cheat on her. But Everardo starts getting so tangled up in his own fiction that, when he wants to put an end to the fantasy, he can no longer escape it.
