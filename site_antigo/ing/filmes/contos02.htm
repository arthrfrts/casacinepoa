*CONTOS DE INVERNO 2002*
(Winter stories 2002)
(Digital Beta, 6 x 20 min, color, 2002)
 

Continuing the teleplay project of Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre in partnership with RBS TV, the new version of "Contos de Inverno", innovating audiovisual fiction in Rio Grande do Sul.

CONTOS DE INVERNO 2002 (WINTER TALES 2002), the new series, is comprised of six episodes with teleplays from Rio Grande do Sul, written especially for television, with local actors, technicians, writers and directors, and also with local themes, settings and spoken in local accents.
