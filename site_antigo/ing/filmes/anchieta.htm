ANCHIETANOS
(Anchietanos)
(Betacam, 46 min, color, 1997)
 
Episode of the series "Comedies of Private Life" of Globo TV.

Chico, Luciano and Cristina are 13 years old and go to the same school, Colégio Anchieta. Chico and Luciano are in love with Cristina, who flirts with both of them alternately. Andrew is a poor kid, he belongs in a different group, but they play each other in a soccer championship. Twenty years later they face each other again, and Chico will have to make the most important decision of his life.

Reviews

"Anchietanos is the story of a group of school friends, left-wingers who, after graduating, stooped to the pragmatism of real life. The bitter-sweet, rather nostalgic and very dense stocktaking of a generation. Whoever saw it was surprised that television could produce such a quality program."
(Luis Zanin Oricchio, O Estado de São Paulo)
