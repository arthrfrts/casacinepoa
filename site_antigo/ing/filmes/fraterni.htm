FRATERNIDADE
(Fraternity)
(Digital Beta, 3 min, color, 2004)
 
Episode of the "Valores do Brazil" (Brazilian Values) series for the 2004 Christmas campaign by Banco do Brasil.

15 years later, a film crew returns to Ilha Grande dos Marinheiros (Sailors’ Great Island), in Porto Alegre, where the ILHA DAS FLORES (Island of Flowes) short movie was shot. And they discuss the possibility of actually helping improve the life of the people living there and surviving off trash collection.
