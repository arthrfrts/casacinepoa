O COMPRADOR DE FAZENDAS
(The Farms buyer)
(HDTV/Digital Beta, 25 min, color, 2001)
 
Episode of the series "Brave People" of Globo TV.

An indebted family tries to sell their decaying property to a film producer, who plans to film an adaptation of Monteiro Lobato’s short story "The farms buyer" and needs an adequate location. Trying to fool the producer, the family disguises the poor conditions of their property.
