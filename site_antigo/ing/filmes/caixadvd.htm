I want to buy the Curtas da Casa
Casa de Cinema's shorts

Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre is launching a box with 4 DVDs. They include over 40 short fiction films, documentaries and TV specials. The films were directed by national and international award winning filmmakers Ana Luiza Azevedo, Carlos Gerbase and Jorge Furtado. The collection also includes the DVD Outras Histórias (Other Stories), with shorts directed by guest filmmakers. All the shorts are subtitled subtitles in four different languages. Exclusive making-of footage and extras.

Historic edition.

Commercial agents can buy CURTAS DA CASA by contacting us through the e-mail distribuidora@casacinepoa.com.br
