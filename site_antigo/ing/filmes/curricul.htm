A IMPORTÂNCIA DO CURRÍCULO NA CARREIRA ARTÍSTICA
(The Importance of the résumé)
(Digital Beta, 23 min, color, 2001)
 
Episode of the series "Winter Stories" of RBS TV.

After four years of Drama School, Ernesto tries to discover what he needs to do to be an actor. Would it be acting in Shakespeare plays? Or doing audition after audition until he is chosen for "that" part on television? Writing a well thought out résumé, with a few little lies or overstated truths may not be proof of any talent, but it may help to be in the right place at the right time.

Reviews

"Ana, who has hit the mark in her films, does it again on television. Her direction is agile, modern and competent. She knows that television is a completely different medium than cinema, aimed at an audience that is infinitely larger than the audience of her films. (...) The entire cast was excellent, counting also with the special participation by the great Suzana Saldanha. But Júlio Andrade and Ana Paula are surprising, because there are no signs that this is their first work on television. They play their characters flawlessly."
(Zeca Kiechaloski, ABC DOMINGO, Novo Hamburgo, 08/07/2001)
