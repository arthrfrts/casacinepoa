TUDO NUM DIA SÓ
(Everything in one day)
(Digital Beta, 23 min, color, 2001)
 
Episode of the series "Winter Stories" of RBS TV.

Paulo is a writer in crisis, struggling to finish a book. His marriage, however, ends suddenly: his wife leaves home leaving only a farewell note. He follows her through the streets and finds strange coincidences between the story he is writing and the one that is happening in front of his eyes.
