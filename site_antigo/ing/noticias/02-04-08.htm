French magazine does 7 pages on Jorge Furtado
(8/Apr/2002)

After the 2nd World Social Forum, Porto Alegre remains a theme in Paris. The magazine BREF, edited in the French capital, is known throughout Europe as "the short films magazine". Issue number 52, which is now on sale (Spring 2002) has a cover headline for a seven page article on Jorge Furtado and the productions of the Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre. The main text, written by Sylvie Delpech, is titled “Jorge Furtado, l'île aux courts" (the island of shorts). There is also a text on the history of the Casa de Cinema, a long interview with Jorge Furtado, a box with his filmography and another one about “Des courts au Brésil” (shorts in Brazil), with mention to the projects Curta nas Telas – Shorts on Screen (by Porto Alegre City Council), Curta às seis – Shorts at six (by Petrobrás) and Curtas gaúchos na TV – Rio Grande do Sul shorts on TV (RBS TV).

The magazine BREF can be found on the web at http://www.agencecm.com/, but only a few of the articles are available on the web.
