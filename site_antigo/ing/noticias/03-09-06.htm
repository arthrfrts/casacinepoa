September is festival month in Europe
(06/Sep/2003)

THE MAN WHO COPIED, a Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre production directed by Jorge Furtado, has already been selected for 3 film festivals that will take place in Europe in September. The film will be screened on the 19th in San Sebastián (Spain), on the 25th in Hamburg (Germany) and on the 29th in Biarritz (France).

The 51st Festival Internacional de Cine Donostia-San Sebastián starts on the September 18th and ends on September 27th. The 6th Film Fest Hamburg takes place from September 19th to 26th . And the 12th Festival des Cinémas et Cultures de l'Amérique Latine La Cita à Biarritz is from September 19th to October 5th.
