The shoot of DONA CRISTINA PERDEU A MEMÓRIA begins
(4/Apr/2002)

Today, April 4th, Ana Luiza Azevedo has begun shooting her new short, DONA CRISTINA PERDEU A MEMÓRIA. The film tells the story of the relationship between an 8 year old boy and an 80 year old woman, in the midst of losses and discoveries.  Dona Cristina is played by Lissy Brock, and the boy, Antônio, is played by Pedro Tergolina, both working in film for the first time. The shoot will go on until Sunday, the 7th, always in Belém Velho, a district of Porto Alegre, and the film should be ready to be entered for the Gramado Film Festival, in June.
