The series "Brava Gente" shows another episode produced by Casa de Cinema
(10/Jul/2001)

The second episode of the Globo TV series "Brava Gente" produced by Casa de Cinema, will be broadcast on the 10th of this month. DIA DE VISITA / VISITING DAY was directed by Ana Luiza Azevedo and the cast includes Malu Mader, Marco Ricca, Marcello Antony, Carlos Cunha Filho and Júlio Andrade. The episode was shot on locations in Porto Alegre. The screenplay, written by Jorge Furtado and Giba Assis Brasil, tells the story of a suburban woman, whose apartment is invaded by a dangerous criminal. The arrival of the policeman in charge of arresting him sparks off an electrifying plot, where nothing is what it seems.
