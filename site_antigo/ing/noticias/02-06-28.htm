HOUVE UMA VEZ DOIS VERÕES (TWO SUMMERS) wins awards in Fortaleza
(28/June/2002)
At the 12th Cine Ceará, which ended on Thursday, June 27th, the film HOUVE UMA VEZ DOIS VERÕES, a Casa de Cinema production, written and directed by Jorge Furtado, won the awards for Best Screenplay, Best Director, Best Editing. The film also won the critics' award for Best Film.

A VIDA EM SEGREDO, by Suzana Amaral, was also voted Best Film by the Official Jury. The other feature film from Rio Grande do Sul in the competition, A FESTA DE MARGARETE, won the Best Soundtrack award.
