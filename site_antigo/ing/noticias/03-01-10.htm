TWO SUMMERS in the summer of Rio de Janeiro
(10/Jan/2003)

After playing in the South, North and Northeast of Brazil and winning 4 awards at the Fortaleza Film Festival, the first feature film by director Jorge Furtado, HOUVE UMA VEZ DOIS VERÕES (TWO SUMMERS) opens in Rio this Friday, January 10th. The film will be showing in three theatres in Rio: Art Fashion
Mall 1, Espaço Unibanco 3 and Estação Ipanema 1.
