SAL DE PRATA (Silver Salt) wins Best Film Editing in Gramado
(20/Aug/2005)

SAL DE PRATA, a Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre production directed by Carlos Gerbase, received the Best Film Editing award at the Thirty-Third Gramado Film Festival - Brazilian and Latin Cinema, which ended this Saturday night, August 19. Pictured are director Carlos Gerbase and star Maria Fernanda Cândido.

SAL DE PRATA opens nationwide September 23, distributed by Columbia. More information about the movie here.
