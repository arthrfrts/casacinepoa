ANTES QUE O MUNDO ACABE (Before the World Ends) selected by SESC
(06/Feb/2006)

SESC Rio has released a list of ten Brazilian citizens, chosen out of 120 who enrolled, for its Movie Script Workshop, which will take place March 14-18, at SESC Nogueira, in Rio de Janeiro. The chosen scripts are: "A Justiça dos Homens", by Paulo Corsino and Péricles Barros (Rio de Janeiro); ANTES QUE O MUNDO ACABE (Before the World Ends), by Ana Luiza Azevedo (Rio Grande do Sul) and Paulo Halm (Rio de Janeiro); "Ao Lado", by Felipe Sholl (Rio de Janeiro); "Bestiário", by Carlos Dowling (Paraíba); "Cemitério de Navios", by Ricardo Favilla (Rio de Janeiro); "Condomínio Jaqueline", by Roberto Moreira (São Paulo), Geórgia Araújo and Sílvia Lourenço (São Paulo); "Domingo no Parque", by Marcos Antônio Lazarini (São Paulo); "É Proibido Fumar", by Anna Muylaert (São Paulo); "O Cabeleira", by Leandro Assis and Hiroshi Maeda (Rio de Janeiro); and "O Sinal", by Wagner de Assis (Rio de Janeiro). Among the consultants were Julie Delpy, actress and writer of "Before Sunset", and Brazilian writer Braulio Mantovani, of "Cidade de Deus" (City of God).
