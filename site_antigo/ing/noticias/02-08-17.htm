DONA CRISTINA wins two awards in Gramado
(17/Aug/2002)

The short DONA CRISTINA PERDEU A MEMÓRIA (DONA CRISTINA HAS LOST HER MEMORY), a Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre production, won the awards for Best Director (Ana Luiza Azevedo) and Best Art Direction (Fiapo Barth) at the 30th edition of the Gramado Film Festival. The Best Short Film award went to "Como se morre no cinema", by Luelane Corrêa.
