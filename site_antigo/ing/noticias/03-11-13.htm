CENA ABERTA starts this Tuesday
(13/Nov/2003)

CENA ABERTA, the most recent co-production of Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre with Globo TV, starts on Friday, 18th at 10:30 PM. The four episode series is presented by Regina Casé and combines fiction and documentary.

The first episode is A HORA DA ESTRELA (THE HOUR OF THE STAR), based on the novel by Clarice Lispector. The cast includes Regina Casé, who presents and acts in all four episodes, and the actors Wagner Moura and Ana Paula Bouzas.

Next episodes:

Nov/11th: NEGRO BONIFÁCIO, based on the short story by Simões Lopes Neto, with Carolina Dieckmann and Lázaro Ramos.

Dec/2nd: FOLHETIM, based on the novel Ópera de Sabão, by Marcos Rey, with Márcio Garcia.

Dec/9th: AS TRÊS PALAVRAS DIVINAS, based on the short story "What Men Live By", by Leo Tolstoy, with Luis Carlos Vasconcelos.
