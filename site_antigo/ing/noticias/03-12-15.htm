THE MAN WHO COPIED wins three more international awards
(15/Dec/2003)

The seventh edition of the Festival Luso Brasileiro de Cinema ended yesterday (Dec. 12th) in Santa Maria da Feira, Portugal. THE MAN WHO COPIED, a production of the Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre, directed by Jorge Furtado, was voted best film by the Popular Jury, by the Critics and by the Federation of Film Clubs.

On Thursday (Dec. 12th) Lázaro Ramos received the Best Actor Award at the Havana Film Festival, in Cuba.
