DONA CRISTINA wins awards in Internet Festival
(2/Dec/2002)

The short film DONA CRISTINA PERDEU A MEMÓRIA (DONA CRISTINA HAS LOST HER MEMORY), directed by Ana Luiza Azevedo, won the best film award in the category "e-cinema" (competition of fiction, experimental and video-art shorts), at the 3rd Fluxus, International Film Festival on the Internet, promoted by Zeta Filmes, Belo Horizonte, which ended on December 1st.

Other award winners were "Kunstbrr", by Steve Whitehouse, Canada (Best Animation), "Mini Cine Tupy", by Sérgio Bloch, Brazil (Best Documentary) and "Estranhas Alegações", by Pedro Paulo Villela, Brazil (Popular Jury).

The award winning films and the other films in the competition can still be seen on http://www.fluxusonline.com.
