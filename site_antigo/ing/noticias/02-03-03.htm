VERDES ANOS (GREEN YEARS), on the 5th, opens the Gaucho Festival on Canal Brasil
(3/Mar/2002)

To commemorate the 230th anniversary of Porto Alegre in March (26th), Canal Brasil will have a special season of films from Rio Grande do Sul (shorts and features), as well as interviews and special reports.

The schedule of the showing of the features is as follows: VERDES ANOS (GREEN YEARS) 5th (Tuesday) at 11:45 pm ; ME BEIJA (KISS ME) 7th (Thursday) at 11:30pm  ANAHY DE LAS MISIONES 12th  (Tuesday) at 11:30pm  FELICIDADE É... (HAPPINESS IS...) 14th (Thursday) at 11:30pm AQUELES DOIS (THOSE TWO) 21st (Thursday) at 11:30pm
