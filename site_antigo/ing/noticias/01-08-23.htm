4 more awards for THE SANDWICH
(23/Aug/2001)

At the 2nd Latin American Film, Video and Television Festival of Campo Grande, Brazil, the short film THE SANDWICH was awarded the Best Fiction Prize. It also won Best Screenplay and Direction, and Felipe Mônaco won Best Actor. The awards ceremony took place in Campo Grande on August 20th.
