SAL DE PRATA (Silver Salt) opens March 17 in Rio
(16/Mar/2006)

Audiences in Rio de Janeiro, Fortaleza, Salvador, Uberlândia, Ribeirão Preto and Manaus will finally be able to watch SAL DE PRATA, a Casa de Cinema feature film directed by Carlos Gerbase. Six months after it opened in Porto Alegre and São Paulo, the movie with Maria Fernanda Cândido, Camila Pitanga, Marcos Breda, Bruno Garcia, Nelson Diniz, Júlio Andrade and Janaína Kramer premieres in other cities around the country.

It opens this Friday, March 17.
