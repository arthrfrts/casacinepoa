WINTER STORIES in editing
(12/Jun/2001)

The four CONTOS DE INVERNO / WINTER STORIES produced by Casa de Cinema in partnership with RBS TV are being edited now. A Importância do Currículo na Carreira Artística (The importance of the resumé in the actor´s career), Jogos do Amor e do Acaso (Games of Love and Chance), Tudo num só dia (Everything in One Day) and O Amante Amador (The Amateur Lover) were shot in April and May in Porto Alegre and they will be shown by RBS TV this month to the entire state of Rio Grande do Sul.
