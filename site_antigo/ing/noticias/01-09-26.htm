The shoot of THE MAN WHO COPIED has started
(26/Sep/2001)

On Monday, September 24th, Jorge Furtado started shooting his new feature, “The Man Who Copied”, in Porto Alegre. In the next seven weeks you can find information on this site about the crew, cast and the shoot, with daily updates by the Studiorama team.
