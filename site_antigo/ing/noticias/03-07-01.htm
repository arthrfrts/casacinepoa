THE MAN WHO COPIED goes into its third week and remains in the top ten box office hits in Brazil
(01/Jul/2003)

In less than 20 days in the theatres, THE MAN WHO COPIED has already been seen by 236,474 viewers and remains among the top ten box office hits in Brazil. The average audience numbers per copy of "The Man Who Copied" puts it in the third place among the films now on in Brazilian theatres (836), coming after "Bruce Almighty" (1,127) and the opening of "Hulk" (1,088). Confirming the excellent word-of-mouth publicity, THE MAN WHO COPIED has kept the average audiences of the previous week and was the picture with the smallest drop in audience numbers among all films being shown in theatres now. The movie is still on in theatres throughout Brazil.
