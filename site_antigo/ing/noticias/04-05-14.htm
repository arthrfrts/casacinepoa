The shoot of MEU TIO MATOU UM CARA / MY UNCLE KILLED A GUY begins
(14/May/2004)

Jorge Furtado’s third feature film, MY UNCLE KILLED A GUY starts shooting this Friday, May 14th 2004, in Porto Alegre. The film tells the story of a teenager, Duca (Darlan Cunha, who played Laranjinha in “City of Men”), and his friends Kid (Renan Gioelli) and Isa (Sophia Reis), as he tries to help his uncle (Lázaro Ramos) who has been accused of murder. Of course, Duca is in love with Isa, and she is in love with Kid. And of course, uncle Eder’s crime has something to do with his girlfriend, Soraya (Deborah Secco). The cast also includes Dira Paes (Kika, from “Amarelo Manga”) and Ailton Graça (Majestade, from “Carandiru”), playing Duca’s parents.
 
This movie is the first co-production of Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre and Paula Lavigne’s production company, Natasha Filmes, from Rio de Janeiro. The executive production is by Nora Goulart and Luciana Tomasi, the photography by Alex Sernambi, art direction by Fiapo Barth and musical direction is by Caetano Veloso. The shoot will last 6 weeks. The film will be released in January 2005.

During the shoot, the Terra website will host a blog with daily updates and exclusive images from the sets and behind the scenes of this Casa de Cinema production. Go to http://www.terra.com.br/blog/meutiomatou1kra/.
