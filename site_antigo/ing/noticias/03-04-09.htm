DONA CRISTINA is the best film of the year in Rio Grande do Sul
(09/Apr/2003)

The short film DONA CRISTINA LOST HER MEMORY, directed by Ana Luiza Azevedo and produced by Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre was chosen Best 35mm Film by the jury of the 3ª Mostra APTC de Cinema Gaúcho (3rd APTC Gaucho Film Festival) The jury was formed by 14 professionals and by the members of APTC (Professional Association of Film Technicians of Rio Grande do Sul). DONA CRISTINA LOST HER MEMORY also received awards for Best Director (Ana Luiza Azevedo) and Best Screenplay (Ana Luiza Azevedo, Jorge Furtado and Rosângela Cortinhas). The 3ª Mostra APTC de Cinema Gaúcho was a great success. The 7 days of screenings brought in 1,700 viewers.

The awards:

Best 35mm film
DONA CRISTINA LOST HER MEMORY, by Ana Luiza Azevedo

Best 16 mm film
(three winners) JUSTIÇA INFINITA, by Cacá Nazario; MIOPIA, by Muriel Paraboni; and A VINGANÇA DE KALI GARA, by Jerri Dias

Best super-8 film
APRIL, by Gustavo Jahn

Best director
Ana Luiza Azevedo (director) and Alfredo Barros (assistant director) for DONA CRISTINA LOST HER MEMORY

Best screenplay
Ana Luiza Azevedo, Jorge Furtado and Rosângela Cortinhas for DONA CRISTINA LOST HER MEMORY

Best actor
Júlio Andrade, in MIOPIA

Best actress
Patsy Cecato, in ISAURA; and Deborah Finocchiaro, in POIS É, VIZINHA

Best music
Fernando Basso, for DOMINGO

Melhor sound
Cristiano Scherer (sound design), Rodrigo Frota (sound recordist), Fernando Basso (sound editing), Eduardo Mayrinck (mixing) and Celso (foley sound effects) in DOMINGO

Best editor
Milton do Prado (editor) and Luís Otávio Feldens (digital editing) in DOMINGO

Best art director
Iara Noemi (art director), Alcimar Bairros (set design), Fabiana Pizetta (costumes), Felipe Loboruk (make-up) and Alessandra Marder (assistant art director) in DOMINGO

Best cinematography
Mauro Pinheiro Jr. (director of photography), Juliano Lopes (assistant cameraman), Alberto La Salvia (2nd assistant cameraman) in DOMINGO

Special Awards
to TWO SUMMERS and MARGARETTE’S PARTY.

Milton Barragan Award (special prize for lifetime achievement)
to ANTÔNIO CARLOS TEXTOR
