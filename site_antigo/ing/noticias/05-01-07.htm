185 thousand viewers in one week
(07/Jan/2005)

MEU TIO MATOU UM CARA/MY UNCLE KILLED A GUY closes its first week on Brazilian cinemas had 185,424 viewers! Considering only the 1st week, the film is 58% above O HOMEM QUE COPIAVA/THE MAN WHO COPIED, Jorge Furtado’s previous feature. Between Monday and Thursday, MY UNCLE… was seen by 117.097 viewers, 106% above THE MAN WHO COPIED for the same period (which totaled 700 thousand viewers).
