Casa de Cinema launches its projects for 2001
(26/3/2001)

Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre is offering cocktail for the press, starting at 7 pm at its headquarters (rua Miguel Tostes, 860 - Porto Alegre), where it will be launching its projects for the first year of the new millennium, including two feature films and six episodes for television. All the projects are already in pre-production and their filming dates are set.
