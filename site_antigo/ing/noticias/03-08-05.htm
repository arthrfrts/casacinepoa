Over half a million viewers
(05/Aug/2003)

On cinema screens for almost two months, THE MAN WHO COPIED is back in the 10 top box office hits in Brazil in the last weekend. Over half a million viewers (528.953) have already seen the film, which is still on in 57 film theatres. Breaking the 500 thousand viewers barrier, THE MAN WHO COPIED becomes one of the 20 Brazilian films with the largest audiences in the past 5 years. Since 1999, only four films had between 500 and 800 thousand viewers.

Brazilian film audiences, 1999 - 2003 (until July).

less than 100 thousand viewers = 116 films
between 100 and 250 thousand viewers = 12 films
between 250 and 500 thousand viewers = 9 films
between 500 and 800 thousand viewers = 4 films
over 800 thousand viewers = 17 films

Total films released = 158
