UM DOS TRÊS (One of the Three) premieres today on "Fantástico"
(06/Nov/2005)

UM DOS TRÊS is a segment of "Fantástico" TV magazine, premiering this Sunday, November 6. It is produced by Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre.

Three people tell the same story to the camera, buy only one of them actually lived the tale. Which one? That is the challenge of "Um dos três", premiering this Sunday on "Fantástico". The audience will vote through phone numbers and the Internet.

Direction supervision and writing by Jorge Furtado. Márcio Schoenardie does scene direction, assisted by Janaína Fischer. Luciana Tomasi and Nora Goulart are the executive producers.
