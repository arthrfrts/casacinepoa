The Man who would not fall
(11/Jul/2003)

At the end of the fourth week of exhibition of THE MAN WHO COPIED, it has already been seen by 341,813 viewers. Audience numbers have INCREASED from the previous week. The average drop in movie attendance numbers is over 30%. THE MAN WHO COPIED, however, was seen by 71,659 viewers in the third week and 72,132 in the fourth week. The film is still being shown in movie theatres throughout Brazil and, even if audience numbers drop in the fifth week, it is safe to say that the movie will reach 400 thousand viewers.
