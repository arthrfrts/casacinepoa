The Man who kept going up
(08/Jul/2003)

Check it out! In its fourth weekend in movie theatres THE MAN WHO COPIED, audience numbers have gone up from the previous weekend. It is rare for a film to see its audience numbers raise, the average drop is over 30%. Just to have an idea, from the second to the third weekend, THE MAN WHO COPIED saw the smallest drop in audiences among all films on, 24%. Now, from the third to the fourth week, the film’s audience numbers have gone UP by 2%. The number of theatres showing the film throughout Brazil has also gone up from 46 to 51. "The Man Who Copied" has already been seen by 308,755 viewers.
