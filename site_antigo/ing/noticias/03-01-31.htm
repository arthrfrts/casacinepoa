CONTRACAMPO magazine has special feature about Casa de Cinema
(31/Jan/2003)

The cyber magazine CONTRACAMPO , edited by Eduardo Valente and Ruy Gardnier, has just released its 47th issue, with a special feature on the 15th anniversary of the Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre.

As well as an interview with Jorge Furtado, the special feature of the month also includes articles by Bolívar Torres (on TWO SUMMERS), Fernando Mascarello (on the presence of the sea in the films of Rio Grande do Sul, and on the “noir” character of TOLERÂNCIA), Ruy Gardnier ("The constructive cinema of Jorge Furtado"), Flávia Seligman (on the super-8 production of RS), Daniel Caetano (on the young comedies of manners of RS), Fabiano de Souza (on DEUS EX-MACHINA), Alfredo Manevy (on the TV special ANCHIETANOS) and Marcus Mello (on the shorts TRÊS MINUTOS, O OITAVO SELO and O VELHO DO SACO).
