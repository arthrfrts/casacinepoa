A tribute to Jorge Furtado in "Brasil dos Gaúchos"
(20/Aug/2004)

From August 17th to September 12th, in Rio de Janeiro, the project "Brasil dos Gaúchos", will be bringing the culture of Rio Grande do Sul to the Centro Cultural dos Correios (Visconde de Itaboraí 20, Rio de Janeiro). The program has several cultural activities, including Music, Film, Literature, Theatre, Art, Comics, Cuisine, Video and others, animated by the participation of artists from Rio Grande do Sul and guests.

The film festival, starting on the 24th, will show 33 shorts and 10 feature films made in Rio Grande do Sul, including 14 productions by Casa de Cinema de Porto Alegre, and it will pay a special tribute to the films of Jorge Furtado, awarding him the prize "Gaúcho Emérito" on the 28th.
